Develop the High Line Alumni Network

Invest in the creation and promotion of the Alumni Network. This initiative could connect young professionals with multiple resources, explore career development paths and learn about upcoming paid opportunities. The Network could be a tool to promote jobs both at the High Line as well as promote opportunities at another partner institutions.

Strengthening the Alumni Network and ensuring that future High Line Teens can have access to a peer Network could have a meaningful impact in their lives.
