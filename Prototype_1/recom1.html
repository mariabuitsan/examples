Develop the High Line Alumni Network

All Recommendations

Invest in the creation and promotion of the High Line Teen Alumni Network. This initiative will provide an opportunity for young professionals to tap into resources, explore career development and learn about upcoming paid opportunities. Alumni Networks seem key to provide support to young job seekers in a critical age as they transition to the workforce.

Strengthening the Alumni Network and ensuring that future High Line Teens can have access to a peer Network could have a meaningful impact in their lives.
